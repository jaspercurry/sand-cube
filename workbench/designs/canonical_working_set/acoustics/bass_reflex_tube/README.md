# 40 mm bass-reflex tube

## Preserved baseline

The rear-corner generator is the present route owner. It preserves a circular,
continuous 40 mm bore, separately printable internal tube, serviceable tower,
rear-corner path, and outlet flare lineage.

Historical diagnostics record a `526.406968 mm` complete physical centerline,
`553.828873 mm` effective length, and `39.208938 Hz` calculated alignment for
that build. These are provenance values, not final inputs.

## Resume gate

Do not change the route until the relevant enclosure variant has accepted
Python source, STEP artifacts and hashes, net acoustic volume, wall/brace/floor
geometry, service opening, print orientation, and installation sweep.

At resumption, define physical and effective lengths consistently, rerun
tuning, and check driver, wall, brace, baffle/hatch, fill-path, floor, tower,
and installation clearances. Prove a continuous 40 mm bore with zero material
intrusion.
