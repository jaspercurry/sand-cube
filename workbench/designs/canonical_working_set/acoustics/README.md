# Acoustic components

The bass-reflex tube and D-squat resonator are separate lineages that will
eventually be packaged together. They remain deliberately outside the current
enclosure refactor.

- The [tube](bass_reflex_tube/README.md) owns the continuous 40 mm airway,
  centerline, flares, serviceable tower, and bass-reflex alignment.
- The [resonator](d_squat_resonator/README.md) is a removable side-branch
  cartridge whose placement and acoustic calibration must be reconciled.

Neither component may be resized or positioned against a temporary enclosure.
The final enclosure volume, walls, braces, floor, baffle/hatch, keepouts, and
installation path are required inputs.
