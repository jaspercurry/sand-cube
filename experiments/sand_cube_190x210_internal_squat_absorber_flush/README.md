# Rear-flush internal squat absorber variant

This isolated variant derives from `sand_cube_190x210_internal_squat_absorber`
without modifying that prior design or the standalone absorber source.

The complete D-flat of the separately printable absorber follows the rear inner
acoustic face with 0.02 mm nominal assembly relief. The 56 mm absorber straight
therefore runs at constant `y`. A shallow three-dimensional elbow and a rotated
return elbow move the 40 mm circular airway rearward into the unchanged
serviceable tower.

The future absorber mounting tab and enclosure mounting node are intentionally
not included in this pass.
