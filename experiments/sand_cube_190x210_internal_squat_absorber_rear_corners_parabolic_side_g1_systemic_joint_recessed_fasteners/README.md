# Systemic joint with recessed fasteners

This isolated sibling keeps the complete parabolic-side G1 enclosure and
replaces the experimental front closure with one coordinated joint on each
printed half:

- one bucket-side system derived from the live 2-3-2 wall stack, with one
  curved gasket land and one continuous printable ramp; and
- one baffle diaphragm from the nested perimeter to the preserved driver
  collar.

There are no independent corner caps, secondary gasket rings, or isolated
baffle webs.

The two M4 fasteners cross the dry outer corner of the joint at 48 degrees
from the top/bottom face normal. Their
head pockets are recessed inside the unchanged top and bottom exterior, and
their compact sleeves are clipped to the authoritative enclosure envelope.
The 9.8 mm pockets allow up to a 9 mm conventional M4 head with assembly
clearance. Each side-loaded square nut sits 6.59 mm behind the baffle bed and
loads against a separate 5 mm structural backstop.

Generated files are written only to the matching directory under `build/`.
