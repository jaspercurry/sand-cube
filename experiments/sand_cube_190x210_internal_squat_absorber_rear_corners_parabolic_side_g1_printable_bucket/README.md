# Parabolic G1 printable bucket and separate baffle

This isolated experimental sibling keeps the complete committed parabolic G1
conformal full system, but changes the enclosure manufacturing split:

- the rear 2-3-2 sand gap is filled to make a solid 7 mm rear wall;
- the side and roof sand cavities remain 2-3-2;
- the main enclosure becomes a rear-bed printable bucket;
- the exact parabolic fairing, black-hole recess, driver collar, and front
  brace roots become a separate front baffle;
- the visible seam follows the exact G1 fairing-to-flat-wall tangent boundary;
- a hidden tapered interface runs from that boundary to a 3.5 mm inset
  perimeter on the driver-collar rear plane;
- 0.30 mm hidden fit clearance fades to zero at the visible seam.

The baffle's driver collar and inset perimeter are coplanar for printing.  The
worst corner of the tapered interface is 40 degrees from the print axis, so it
does not require a horizontal support ceiling.
